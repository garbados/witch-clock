# Q & A

Riddles confronted.

## Why?

Because I want to observe the sun and moon and stars, and honor that I live on a chaotic orchestra so much bigger than me. Because I do not want precise mathematical leap-year rules. I want months that are longer or shorter because the moon does as it pleases; seasons that are longer or shorter because the sun is not a piece of clockwork but a fiery expression of Creation's unknowable majesty, subject to forces that can only be accounted for after they make themselves known.

Because I live on Earth, the magnificent paradise.
