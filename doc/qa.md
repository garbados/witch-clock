## Why?

Because I was sick and tired of pretending the Gregorian, a Catholic calendar, could be separated from the violent, vicious history of the theocratic empires known collectively as Christendom, and its modern incarnation, Whiteness. I do not want to celebrate Christmas, or New Years, because Pope Greg told my ancestors they must, or must be slaughtered. I want to observe the sun and moon and stars, and honor that I live not on some trinity's bequeathed gift but a chaotic orchestra so much bigger than me, that summoned *me* for *it*, not the other way around.

Because I do not want precise mathematical leap-year rules. I want months that are longer or shorter because the moon does as it pleases; seasons that are longer or shorter because the sun is not a piece of clockwork but a fiery expression of Creation's unknowable majesty, subject to forces that can only be accounted for after they make themselves known.

Because I live on Earth, the magnificent paradise.
